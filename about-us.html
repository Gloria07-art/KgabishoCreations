About Us

Background

My name is Tshepisho Mabija the founder of Kgabisho Creations. I started knitting as hobby in 2017 and
recievd lots of compliments for my distinct patterns. I decided start selling crochet items in 2022 as my
side hustle and promoted it on social media. We received an overwheming amount of orders around the country.
The name "Kgabisho" is derived from the Setswana word "Beautify". We hope to grow.

Crochet items

Our creations range from wearables to decorations and blankets. A customer usually shows what they desire to be
knitted and then we knit their dreams to life.
The customer must be very specific on what they desire to be knitted. The prices differ on what you want and the type
of pattern and wool.

Knitting Classes

We hold physical and online knitting classes. We teach different designs and intricate patterns. We aim to give quality training with spacial inidividual attention. Most take part as a stress relieving activity and others learn the skill to establish their qwn businesses.We would like to partner with oganiztions in the future to train their employees and distribute the creations to charity.
