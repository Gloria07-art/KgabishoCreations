Knitting lessons

Kgabisho Creations is a Kimberley based business and
was founded in 2022. Our main and initial mission was to bring
fashion, happiness and colour to the nation specifically our based region.
We have evolved from selling basic yet cute crochet items ranging from
blankets, dolls, clothes etc to empowering people through offering high
quality crochet lessons.
We also donate the creations of work to charity. We as Kgabisho Creations
offer cute crochet items, quality lessons and business tips.
